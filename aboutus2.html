About Our Company

The goal of TechConnect is to provide people a way to improve their technical skills and help
them find employment opportunities. In addition, this also aims to help people gain skills
that will be invaluable to whatever positions they are seeking.

Find Employment

Employment opportunities in companies like Google, Microsoft, Twitter, and Facebook. Apply to companies who are seeking people with YOUR skills.

Improving Skills

Those unfamiliar with certain skills will be able to find classes and resources to learn and/or ameliorate those skills, allowing them to open doors to more job opportunities and prospects.

Provide Equal Access

One distinct feature of this resource is an internet access locator, so those without regular access to the internet at home are still able to connect elsewhere.
